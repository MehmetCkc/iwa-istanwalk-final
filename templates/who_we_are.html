WHO WE ARE

An independent note from the streets, ferries, hills, markets, and meeting points of Istanbul.

Vol. 01 Printed for curious feet Istanbul, Today

We are local walkers, route makers, story collectors, and Istanbul obsessives. Istanwalk exists for people who want the city without a glass wall between them and the street.

Our tours are built around neighborhoods, ferry habits, old stones, market counters, late light, ordinary corners, and the guides who know how to make those details speak.

No glossy performance. No empty checklist. Just Istanbul at walking speed.

We keep the walks direct, human, and grounded. The routes are strong, the meeting points are clear, and the guides care about where they are taking you.

Every walk is made for people who would rather notice a doorway, a ferry habit, a food counter, or a street corner than rush through a list.
